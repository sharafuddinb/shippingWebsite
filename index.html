How it works

Transparent milestones keep your shipment on track.

  1. 1. Request a tailored quote

    Share your vehicle details, pickup zip code, and preferred arrival date. We provide a written quote within 24 hours.

  2. 2. Schedule pickup & inspection

    Your dedicated coordinator arranges carrier pickup, completes a condition report, and confirms transit insurance.

  3. 3. Ocean transit to Dubai

    Vehicles are consolidated at the nearest port and loaded according to your shipping method.

  4. 4. Final delivery or forwarding

    We coordinate port clearance in Dubai and overland forwarding to Afghanistan cities including Kabul, Kandahar, and Mazar-i-Sharif.
